AIRTEX

This 200 g/m² light fabric is a 100% polyester fabric, which is coated with acrylate on one side and impregnated. AIRTEX® classic has outstanding properties in comparison with dyed materials made of polyester fabric or cotton.

LOOPKNIT

In loop knitting, long, dangling loops are introduced into the middle of a knitted fabric or along an edge (fringe border). An overall array of such loops may be used to give a "shaggy" look to the fabric and/or make it warmer, e.g., tufting the inside of mittens.

HONEY COMB

Honeycomb Knit is a solid color knit fabric with a raised honeycomb design. Available in many colors. Before cutting, we recommend washing and drying this fabric in the same manner that you will use to launder the finished garment.
